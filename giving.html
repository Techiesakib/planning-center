Flexible Donation Options

People can choose how they want to give.

  • Mobile App: Provide donors with a seamless giving experience on the free Church Center app.

  • Online: Link to or embed the online interface where people can give via card, ACH bank transfer, or Apple Pay.

  • Text-to-Give: Encourage spontaneous giving by accepting instant donations via text message.

  • Cash and Check: Count batches of physical donations quickly and accurately, or use a check reader for extra speed.

Insightful Donation Data

Understand donation activity with comprehensive reporting.

  • Dashboard: Get an overview of every aspect of donation activity in a dynamic, interactive report.

  • Filterable List: See donations as they come in on a live-updating feed, and filter the list to look at specific subgroups.

  • Donation Lifecycle: Zoom in on the details of individual donations to learn the source, deposit status, and more.

  • Monthly Forecasting: Estimate next month’s income using recurring donation trends.

Connected Donor Profiles

Giving history, contact information, and communication
options all in one place

  • Contact Information: Donors' information is synced across Planning Center, keeping your database accurate.

  • Profiles: View a complete picture of each donor’s giving history, no matter how they give.

  • Statements: Create accurate and complete statements with ease and deliver to donors either digitally or by mail.

  • Donor Pledges: Track individual giving commitments from donors towards campaigns and share their progress in statements.
