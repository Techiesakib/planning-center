Know Your People

Keep everything you need to know about your congregation in their
member profiles.

  • View people's participation in the church, like group attendance and giving records, on an activity feed.

  • Order background checks on potential volunteer and staff members using our integration with Checkr.

  • Keep track of any details you want, like baptism dates or serving interests, by creating custom profile fields

Connect With People

Online forms to help you hear directly from people in your church.

  • Get people involved by creating forms for things like volunteer signups or becoming a member.

  • Make digital connect cards to collect visitor information and keep regular attendee profiles current.

  • Give people a way to request prayer and automatically send requests to your prayer team.

Follow Up With People

Custom workflows you and your team can make to reach out to people.

  • Walk people step-by-step through a process, like volunteer training or becoming a member.

  • Collaborate with your team by sharing notes and assigning tasks as you walk people through the process.

  • Set due dates and reminders to ensure your team equips and supports people at every step.

Find People

Create unlimited list reports to find groups of people within your church.

  • Filter through the information you have stored throughout Planning Center to find groups of people.

  • Add everyone from your list to a workflow for you to use to follow up with later.

  • Send mass emails to everyone on your list using a template or writing a custom message.
