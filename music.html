Sheet Music

Customize your songbook and share with your team.

  • Notes: Add annotations to sheet music by highlighting, drawing, typing text, or importing someone else’s notes.

  • Editing: Arrange pages in any order, and use only the sections you need by zooming in and cropping.

Rehearsal Tools

Work on your part by following along with the music.

  • Page-Turning: Turn through sheet music by swiping, scrolling, or stepping with a foot pedal as you play.

  • Metronome: Play in time to the tempo of each song with an audible click or visual flash.

  • Audio Player: Listen to audio files attached to songs and loop sections you want to practice.

Collaboration Options

Lead a unified team by providing musicians with clear visual direction.

  • Synced Displays: Allow one person to wirelessly control multiple devices and turn through pages for everyone..

  • Secondary Displays: Turn any TV into a large dual-page stand using an AppleTV, Chromecast, or a video out cable.
