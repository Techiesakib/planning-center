Publishing + Church Center

Church Center is a mobile app and web experience where your congregation can explore, engage, and get involved throughout the week. Publishing is how you make Church Center your own by creating and sharing custom content.

Home Page & Navigation

Welcome people to Church Center with a custom landing
page—highlight important details, make announcements,
whatever you want!

You can also set the name and icon of built-in pages from
the Planning Center products you subscribe to, and link
to outside pages from the navigation icons.

Free
Custom Pages

Design and publish unique web pages for Church Center
by uploading your own images, formatting text, and
selecting the navigation icon from a library of options.

$14/mo
Custom Pages & Sermons

Broadcast your services live in Church Center, either
from a livestream or a prerecorded video, so people can
tune in and chat as they watch.

You can also link to your recorded sermon and video
library on Youtube and Vimeo for people to watch on
their own time.

$29/mo
