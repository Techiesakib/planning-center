Define the Details

Collect and organize the information you need from attendees.

  • Attendee Types: Create attendee types, like Volunteer or Child with unique pricing and requirements.

  • Add-Ons: Give attendees the option to purchase extra items or activities, like a t-shirt or snowboarding.

  • Questions and Forms: Create custom question fields for each attendee type, or require a specific form (like a medical release).

Simplify Signups

Give people a clear and easy path to sign up and participate.

  • Event Page: Send people to your website or the Church Center mobile app to find and sign up for events..

  • Payment: Accept online and in-person payments, receive partial deposits, issue refunds, and offer discounts and scholarships.

  • Campus and Category Filters: Help people find events close by and relevant to their interests..

Coordinate Attendees

Make your event a success by giving attendees a smooth experience.

  • Assignments: Manually assign people to groups (like vans, tables, cabins) or auto-assign with pre-set filters.

  • Communication: Send individual email reminders to attendees or contact everyone at once.

  • Event Check-in: Check people in and direct them to their assigned location with Check-Ins.
