Fast and Smooth

Customize your check-in process by using as many stations as you want.

  • Manned Station: Enter new household information on the spot, or look regulars up by name.

  • Roster Station: Run check-in "clipboard style" off lists of people assigned to a specific classroom.

  • Self Station: Families can quickly check themselves in using a phone number or scanning a barcode on the Church Center app.

Safe and Secure

Give parents and guardians confidence in the safety of their children.

  • Security Label: Match the code on children's name tags to their guardian's labels to ensure safe pick-up..

  • Emergency Texts: Need a diaper changed, have a medical emergency, or want to tell a parent everything is ok? Send them a text.

  • Trusted People: See who is and who is not allowed to pick up children in their individual profile notes.

  • Reports: Know the exact time children are picked up as well as who picked them up.

Flexible Setup

Use the technology you want to check in and print your own custom labels.

  • Custom Labels: Pour some creativity into it—anything from your logo to custom data fields. You could even make a birthday label!

  • Mobile App + Printers: Turn any iOS or Android device into a station, and print labels on Citizen or Brother printers.

  • Desktop App + Printers: Use a Mac or Windows computer as a station, and print labels on Dymo or Zebra printers.
