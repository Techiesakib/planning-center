Volunteer Scheduling

Schedule people for roles in every ministry in your church.

  • Teams: Schedule people according to their assigned team positions , like Usher on the Hospitality team.

  • Calendar Preferences: Schedule people according to their specified preferences, block out dates, conflicts, and declined requests.

  • Communication: Send scheduling requests to people via text, email, or push notifications on the mobile app.

  • Advance Scheduling: Schedule months ahead using The Matrix, and fill in any remaining positions with Auto-Scheduling..

Worship Planning

Prepare your team for services with an organized, detailed plan.

  • Service Plans: Create unique plans for each service, including details on people, songs, and notes.

  • Service Types: File plans under ministry categories, like Children’s Ministry, or by services, like Contemporary.

  • Order of Service: Set the order and length of each item in the service so your team knows what to expect.

  • Services LIVE: Show your team which item is happening during the service, record timing, and chat! Watch this video to see it in

Rehearsal Tools

Equip your musicians to practice songs in advance and collaborate.

  • Transposing: Help musicians practice songs in the right key by transposing chord charts and audio files.

  • Song Library: Upload your own songs or import from 3rd-party apps and include arrangement details.

  • Rehearsal Files: Attach chord charts, audio files, and videos to the service plan and share with your team.

  • Mobile Media Player: Musicians can listen to audio files, loop trick sections, or watch videos using the mobile app.
